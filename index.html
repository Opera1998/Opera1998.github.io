Utah

Utah is a state in the western Unites States. It's boarded by Colorado to the East, Wyoming to the Northwest, Idaho to the North, Arizona to the South, & Nevada to the West. It also touches a corner of New Mexico in the Southeast. The state is a center of Transportation, Education, Information Technology, & Research, And a major tourist destination for outdoor recreation. Salt Lake City, the state capital & largest city, is home to the headquarters of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter day Saints (LDS Church).
