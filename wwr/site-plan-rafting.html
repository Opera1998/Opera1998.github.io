purpose

The purpose of a rafting business is to offer recreational activities for consumers who enjoy water sports and the thrill of whitewater1. Rafting businesses can offer single or multi-day experiences for families and can be advertised to the general public, schools, corporations, and clubs to gain customers2. Rafting businesses can be profitable and provide adventure trips that almost anyone can participate in

audience

There are 10 reasons why people would want to go rafting & we'll list 3 of them. Do you want to increase your arm muscles by rowing? Are you a fan of animals but want to see them up front? Do you like learning about certain areas of the outdoors? Then Come on down to the Lewis & Clark rafters. Our rafting Oars can increase your strength as well as get the blood pumping. Our guides will tell you some interesting facts that you may not have known. If you look carefully, you might even stumble across a rare species of Trout, Bear, Eagle, & more.
